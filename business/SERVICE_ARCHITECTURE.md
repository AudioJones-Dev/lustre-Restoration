# Lustre Surface Restoration — Service Architecture

## Purpose

This document defines the service catalog architecture for Lustre Surface Restoration so the company can launch narrowly, validate demand, and expand deliberately without confusing the market.

## Service architecture principle

The public brand is broad. The active offer should stay narrow until operational capability is proven.

Do not sell a service line until Lustre has:

- Training path
- Material/equipment plan
- Insurance review
- Written scope language
- Quality-control checklist
- Before/after proof
- Warranty boundaries
- Pricing model

## Launch division: Surface Wrap Division

### Primary offer

Countertop and cabinet wrapping for residential, rental, STR, and commercial refresh projects.

### Core services

| Service | Launch status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Countertop wrapping | Active launch wedge | Best visual proof and easiest market education path. |
| Cabinet wrapping | Active launch wedge | Pairs naturally with countertop wrapping. |
| Bathroom vanity wrapping | Active secondary service | Smaller ticket but good portfolio generator. |
| Backsplash surface film | Optional add-on | Only if substrate and heat exposure are suitable. |
| Commercial architectural film | Selective pilot | Use for reception desks, counters, office surfaces, and retail fixtures. |
| Rental/STR refresh package | Active B2B package | Best repeat-buyer wedge. |

### Launch package structure

#### Investor Refresh

Designed for landlords, STR hosts, flippers, and property managers.

- Cost-controlled finishes
- Fast turnaround
- Repeatable finish library
- Volume-pricing path
- Photo-based quote intake

#### Signature Surface Wrap

Designed for homeowners and condo owners.

- Better finish selection
- Material samples
- Written workmanship warranty
- Before/after documentation

#### Premium Architectural Finish

Designed for luxury residential and commercial clients.

- Premium finish library
- Higher scrutiny on material selection
- Commercial scope documentation
- Longer consultation and approval process

## Offer boundary rules

### What Lustre can say during launch

- Countertop and cabinet wrapping
- Surface refresh without replacement
- Fast photo quote
- South Florida surface restoration brand
- Future-ready surface services

### What Lustre should not claim yet

- Full-service floor restoration provider
- Stone restoration expert
- Certified marble polishing provider
- Commercial floor maintenance provider
- Any manufacturer certification not actually held

## Service expansion gates

A new service line can be added publicly only when all gates are met:

1. Training complete
2. Supplier/equipment plan complete
3. Pricing model complete
4. Insurance reviewed
5. SOP drafted
6. Quality checklist drafted
7. Warranty language drafted
8. At least 2 pilot jobs documented
9. Before/after assets captured
10. Customer feedback reviewed

## Strategic reason for this architecture

The risk is not that the brand is too broad. The risk is that the active service offer expands faster than capability. This architecture lets Lustre own the broader surface restoration category while validating one profitable wedge at a time.
