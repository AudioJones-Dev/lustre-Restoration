# Supplier Strategy

## Principle

Do not build the business around the cheapest film. Build it around professional-grade material, predictable installation behavior, supplier support, and margin discipline.

## Material tiering

| Tier | Customer fit | Material direction | Positioning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Investor Refresh | STR, landlords, flippers, multifamily | Value architectural film | Budget-efficient, fast, repeatable |
| Signature Surface Wrap | Homeowners, condos | Mid-premium architectural film | Best value, strong finish library |
| Premium Architectural Finish | Luxury residential, offices, hospitality | Premium architectural film | Highest trust, commercial-grade positioning |

## Brands to evaluate

### Premium tier

- 3M DI-NOC
- Cover Styl'

### Value / volume tier

- LX Hausys BENIF
- Bodaq

### Avoid for paid professional work

- Generic Amazon films
- Temu-style peel-and-stick film
- Unbranded material with no spec sheets
- Materials with unclear heat, moisture, or cleaning guidance

## Supplier requirements

Before approving a supplier, confirm:

- Architectural/interior film product line
- Technical data sheets
- Finish sample books
- Cleaning and maintenance guidance
- Warranty terms
- Installer training or certification access
- South Florida shipping speed
- Return and defect policy
- Dealer pricing path
- Net terms path after purchase history

## Procurement strategy

### First 30 days

- Order sample books only
- Buy material job-by-job
- Avoid large inventory commitments
- Track material waste per job

### After validation

- Negotiate installer/dealer pricing
- Keep inventory for top-selling finishes
- Build finish library by segment: rental, homeowner, commercial
- Create material approval form for customers

## Material risk controls

- Do not install on unsuitable substrate
- Require surface prep checklist
- Photograph pre-install defects
- Get customer finish approval before ordering
- Document cleaning restrictions
- Keep warranty language separate for material and workmanship

## Open research tasks

- Identify U.S. and Florida distributors for 3M DI-NOC, Cover Styl', LX Hausys BENIF, and Bodaq
- Compare minimum order quantities
- Compare sample book costs
- Confirm contractor/dealer application process
- Confirm certification options
- Confirm average shipping time to Miami-Dade/Broward/Palm Beach
