# Lustre Surface Restoration

**Premium surface transformations without replacement.**

Lustre Surface Restoration is a South Florida surface renewal brand focused on fast, premium, low-disruption upgrades for kitchens, bathrooms, rentals, multifamily properties, hospitality spaces, offices, and commercial interiors.

## Brand promise

**Restore. Renew. Reveal.**

## Initial wedge

The first go-to-market wedge is **countertop and cabinet wrapping** for customers who want a better-looking space without demolition, replacement timelines, or full remodel pricing.

Initial services:

- Countertop wrapping
- Cabinet wrapping
- Bathroom vanity wrapping
- Rental and Airbnb refresh packages
- Commercial architectural surface film
- Property-manager unit-turn refreshes

## Expansion intent

Lustre is intentionally named **Surface Restoration** so the company can later add stone, tile, grout, terrazzo, concrete, and floor restoration without changing the brand.

Planned future divisions:

- Surface Wrap Division
- Stone and Hard-Surface Restoration Division
- Floor Restoration Division
- Lustre Care maintenance plans

## Category position

Lustre is not positioned as a commodity wrap installer. The category is:

> **Premium Surface Restoration**

The customer does not buy vinyl film. The customer buys a cleaner, faster, lower-risk way to make a surface look new.

## Operating thesis

The installation service is the visible product. The durable advantage should come from:

- Fast quoting
- Visual proof
- Material standards
- Review velocity
- Referral partnerships
- Repeatable installation SOPs
- Clear warranty language
- CRM and follow-up automation
- Property-manager and investor relationships

## Current repo purpose

This repository is the canonical launch workspace for the Lustre Surface Restoration business buildout: brand foundation, validation plan, supplier strategy, sales scripts, pricing, operating SOPs, expansion roadmap, and later the digital quote/CRM stack.
