# Lustre Surface Restoration — Launch Blueprint

## Objective

Validate whether a premium surface restoration offer can acquire profitable South Florida customers before committing meaningful capital.

## Launch offer

**Founding Customer Surface Refresh**

- Free photo quote within 24 hours
- Optional in-home measurement
- Material samples shown before install
- Founding customer discount in exchange for before/after photos and an honest review
- Written workmanship warranty

## Headline options

1. **New kitchen look without a $50K remodel.**
2. **Refresh countertops and cabinets in days, not weeks.**
3. **Turn rentals faster with no demo, no mess, and photo-ready surfaces.**

## Landing page sections

1. Hero: promise, photo upload CTA, click-to-call
2. Problem: dated surfaces make properties look old
3. Solution: surface restoration without replacement
4. Before/after gallery
5. Packages
6. Process
7. Materials and warranty
8. Service areas
9. Referral partner offer
10. Quote form

## Week 1 execution

- Update brand from Floor Restoration to Surface Restoration
- Create one-page landing page
- Create Google Business Profile
- Build 60-prospect list
- Draft outreach scripts
- Prepare sample kit plan
- Prepare before/after creative assets
- Set up tracking sheet

## Week 2 execution

- Launch Meta before/after test
- Contact Airbnb management companies
- Contact property managers
- Contact investor-focused real estate agents
- Publish 3 pieces of content
- Follow up every Week 1 contact

## Week 3 execution

- Push warm leads to quotes
- Book first 3 paid or deposit-ready jobs
- Capture before/after proof
- Request reviews
- Contact multifamily property managers

## Week 4 execution

- Compile metrics
- Score against validation framework
- Decide: strong go, conditional go, or no-go
