# Lustre Surface Restoration — Brand Foundation

## Name

**Lustre Surface Restoration**

## Tagline

**Restore. Renew. Reveal.**

## Primary positioning line

**Premium surface transformations without replacement.**

## Category

Premium Surface Restoration

## Market-facing explanation

Lustre Surface Restoration helps homeowners, landlords, short-term rental operators, property managers, and commercial operators refresh high-visibility interior surfaces without demolition, replacement, or long remodel timelines.

## What the brand should signal

- Premium, not cheap
- Clean, not messy
- Fast, not rushed
- Durable, not temporary
- Transparent, not contractor-vague
- Design-forward, not handyman-generic

## Core customer problem

Customers have dated or damaged surfaces that make a property look old, lower its perceived value, or delay turnover. Full replacement is often too expensive, slow, disruptive, or unnecessary.

## Core promise

Lustre gives customers a visibly renewed space in days instead of weeks, at a fraction of replacement cost, using professional materials, clear scope, and documented workmanship.

## First ICPs

1. Short-term rental operators and co-hosts
2. Buy-and-hold landlords
3. Property managers
4. Fix-and-flip investors
5. Budget-conscious homeowners with dated kitchens
6. Realtors and home stagers preparing listings

## Brand hierarchy

### Company

Lustre Surface Restoration

### Category

Premium Surface Restoration

### Launch wedge

Countertop and cabinet wrapping

### Expansion path

The brand is intentionally broad enough to add floor restoration later. Floor restoration should be treated as a planned division, not the launch wedge.

### Expansion services

- Bathroom vanity restoration
- Interior door and built-in wrapping
- Office and reception desk refinishing
- Elevator interior surface film
- Restaurant bar and counter refreshes
- Hospitality and multifamily common-area refreshes
- Stone restoration
- Tile and grout restoration
- Terrazzo restoration
- Concrete polishing
- Floor restoration
- Maintenance plans under Lustre Care

## Voice and tone

Clear, premium, direct, and visual. Avoid sounding like a bargain handyman. The brand should make customers feel that they are buying a professional upgrade, not a temporary patch.

## Messaging pillars

### 1. No demolition

A better-looking surface without ripping out the existing structure.

### 2. Fast turnaround

Most residential refreshes should be positioned around 1–2 day completion when scope allows.

### 3. Cost control

A premium look at a lower cost than replacement, without pretending every job is cheap.

### 4. Visual proof

Before/after photography is the core conversion asset.

### 5. Professional process

Photo quote, written estimate, material samples, scheduled install, workmanship documentation, review request.

## Naming rule

Use **Lustre Surface Restoration** publicly. Avoid reverting to **Lustre Floor Restoration** because it narrows the brand too early and conflicts with the broader surface-transformation strategy.

## Expansion guardrail

The brand may reference broad surface restoration, but public service claims should only include services Lustre can execute or supervise at a professional standard. Do not market floor restoration until training, equipment, insurance, SOPs, and quality-control standards are in place.
