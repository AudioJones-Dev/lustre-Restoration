# Lustre Surface Restoration — CRM Pipeline

## Purpose

This pipeline turns landing-page quote requests, referral leads, and outbound prospects into tracked opportunities that can be measured against the 30-day validation scorecard.

## Pipeline stages

| Stage | Definition | Exit criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| New Lead | New inbound or outbound response received | Lead reviewed and scored |
| Needs Photos | Lead did not provide enough visual info | Photos received or lead disqualified |
| Qualified | Project appears to fit service, geography, and minimum value | Quote prepared or site visit scheduled |
| Quote Sent | Preliminary or formal quote delivered | Accepted, rejected, or follow-up needed |
| Sample / Scope Review | Customer needs material review, measurement, or scope clarification | Final quote approved or rejected |
| Booked | Customer approved and install date is scheduled | Deposit or written approval received |
| Completed | Work completed | Review/care/referral sequence triggered |
| Won / Closed | Job completed and payment collected | Portfolio/review documentation complete |
| Lost | Customer declined, unqualified, or non-responsive | Lost reason recorded |

## Required lost reasons

- Price too high
- Below minimum job size
- Outside service area
- Service not offered yet
- Surface not suitable
- Customer chose replacement/remodel
- Customer chose competitor
- No response
- Timeline mismatch
- DIY intent
- Other

## Follow-up sequence

### New lead with photos

1. Same day: acknowledge and ask any missing question
2. Same day or next day: send quote range or next-step request
3. Day 3: follow up with sample/process note
4. Day 7: final active follow-up
5. Day 14: nurture with before/after proof

### New lead without photos

1. Immediate: request three photo angles
2. Day 1: reminder with example angles
3. Day 3: final photo request
4. Day 7: move to nurture if no response

### Quote sent

1. Day 1: confirm quote received
2. Day 3: ask what would help them decide
3. Day 7: offer sample review / install window if still interested
4. Day 14: close loop and move to nurture

## Guardrail

If lead volume looks strong but quote quality is weak, do not scale ads. Diagnose whether the issue is wrong segment, unclear offer, poor creative, low-intent traffic, or pricing mismatch.
