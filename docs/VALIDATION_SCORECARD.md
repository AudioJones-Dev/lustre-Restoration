# 30-Day Validation Scorecard

This scorecard governs whether Lustre Surface Restoration should proceed beyond a low-capital validation sprint.

## Decision rule

Do not scale spend, buy heavy equipment, or hire ahead of proof. Use real customer behavior, not enthusiasm.

## Go signals

| Signal | Target | Weight |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Paid jobs booked | At least 3 | 25 |
| Meta cost per lead | At or below $75 | 15 |
| Quote close rate | At or above 25% | 15 |
| Formal referral partner | At least 1 | 12 |
| Average project value | At or above $800 | 10 |
| Organic inbound inquiries | At least 5 | 8 |
| Revenue booked | At least $2,400 | 6 |
| Quotes delivered | At least 10 | 5 |
| Repeat or upsell inquiry | At least 1 | 4 |

## Score interpretation

| Score | Decision | Action |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 70–100 | Strong go | Formalize operations, supplier terms, insurance, and scale outreach. |
| 50–69 | Conditional go | Proceed only after fixing the weak lever and meeting operational prerequisites. |
| Under 50 | No-go | Do not commit capital. Revisit offer, segment, or positioning. |

## Absolute kill criteria

Stop regardless of other signals if either condition happens:

1. Fewer than 3 quotes delivered in 30 days.
2. Average inbound lead quality below $500 project value.

## Conditional-go prerequisites

Proceed only if all are true:

- A qualified installer is available before meaningful volume is accepted.
- A reliable material supplier is selected.
- General liability insurance is active before the first paid job.
- Written scope, warranty, and customer sign-off language exists.
